Game rules

  1. Choose a Funder. Everyone else is a Designer.
  2. The Funder receives a random Pitch (consisting of a Topic and a Requirement); and shows the Pitch to the Designers.
  3. Designers pitch ideas to fulfill the Pitch.
  4. The Funder picks a design winner according to which Designer best fulfills the Pitch.
  5. If the Funder feels difficult to pick, he could get a random Personal Interest Card to help him make the decision.
  6. The Funder role passes to the left.
