Game rules

  1. Choose an Investor. Everyone else is an Exhibit Designer.
  2. The Investor receives a Pitch Card and a Refinement Card; and shows them to the Designers.
  3. The Investor receives a Personal Interest Card which they keep secret from the Designers.
  4. Designers pitch ideas to fulfill the Pitch and Refinement Cards.
  5. The Investor picks a design winner according to the Refinement and Personal Interest Cards.
  6. The Investor role passes to the left.

You are an
Exhibit Designer.

You have to pitch ideas.
Listen to the Investor for
Pitch and Refinement!

30 seconds to think.
